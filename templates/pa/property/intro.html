11-Hour Personal Property & Umbrella Update for Public Adjusters

This course fulfills the state-mandated requirements for the Public Adjuster Optional Electives continuing education component for Florida-licensed public adjusters. (3-20)

In this course we will review some of the fundamental concepts and some of the complex coverage details involving Personal Property, Auto, and Umbrella insurance. Also included will be chapters on Ethical guidlines and Underwriting. In addition, we will also provide answers for the most common questions you may encounter from your clients about these risks.

Our course is 100% online and can be taken in the privacy of your own home at your convenience.

Class highlights include:

  • Insurance Ratings
  • Personal Property Insurance
  • Inland Marine Floaters
  • Watercraft Coverage
  • Auto Insurance Coverages
  • Specialized Coverage
  • Umbrella Coverage
  • Ethics
  • Underwriting

This course has been approved by the Florida Department of Financial Services for insurance continuing education.
