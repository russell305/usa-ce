5-Hour Law & Ethics Update for Public Adjusters

This course fulfills the state-mandated requirements for the Law and Ethics continuing education component for Florida-licensed public adjusters. (5-320)

Florida 5-Hour Law & Ethics Update for Public Adjusters examines the duties and ethical responsibilities of Public Insurance Adjusters in Florida (3-20). It takes a look at the many new rules and regulations that Public Adjusters must comply with when transacting insurance, including ethical situations that may arise. Recent changes to state and federal laws that affect the insurance industry, and disciplinary trends and enforcement actions are also discussed.

Our course is 100% online and can be taken in the privacy of your own home at your convenience.

The course follows the state-mandated outline and includes the following sections:
  1. Regulatory Awareness
  2. Insurance Law and Updates
  3. Ethical Requirements
  4. Disciplinary and Industry Trends

This course has been approved by the Florida Department of Financial Services for insurance continuing education.
