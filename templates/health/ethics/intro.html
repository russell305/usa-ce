Florida 5-Hour Law & Ethics Update For Health Agents (5-240)

Florida 5-Hour Law & Ethics Update for Health examines the duties and ethical responsibilities of Health Agents in Florida (2-40). It takes a look at the many new rules and regulations that Agents must comply with when transacting insurance, including ethical situations that may arise. Recent changes to state and federal laws that affect the insurance industry, and disciplinary trends and enforcement actions are also discussed.

Our course is 100% online and can be taken in the privacy of your own home at your convenience.

The course follows the state-mandated outline for the Law and Ethics continuing education component for Florida-licensed Health Agents. It includes the following sections:
  1. Regulatory Awareness
  2. Ethical Requirements
  3. Insurance Law and Updates
  4. Disciplinary and Industry Trends

This course has been approved by the Florida Department of Financial Services for insurance continuing education.
