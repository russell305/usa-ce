Florida 10-Hour Long-Term Care & Partnership Programs for Life, Health, & Annuity Agents

This course fulfills the state-mandated requirements for the Life, Health, & Annuity Agents Optional continuing education component for Florida Life, Health, & Annuity Agents. (2-15 & 2-18)

Florida 10-Hour Long-Term Care & Partnership Programs for Life, Health, & Annuity Agents examines long-term care and long-term care insurance for Life, Health, & Annuity Agents in Florida . The course covers the nature of long-term care, available long-term care services and providers, long-term care insurance products, government-sponsored health care options, partnership programs, alternative LTC funding options, and the ethical and suitability issues associated with long-term care products and policies.

Our course is 100% online and can be taken in the privacy of your own home at your convenience.

Class highlights include:
  • Conditions that have led to the growing need for long-term care
  • Elder Care Needs
  • Types of long-term care providers and the services they offer
  • Options and limitations of Medicare and Medicaid for paying for long-term care
  • Fundamentals of long-term care partnership programs and their requirements

This course has been approved by the Florida Department of Financial Services for insurance continuing education.
