Florida 9-Hour Medicare & Medicaid CE Update for Life, Health, & Annuiity Agents

This course fulfills the state-mandated requirements for the Life, Health, & Annuity Agents Optional Electives continuing education component for Florida Life, Health, & Annuity Agents. (2-15 & 2-18)

Florida 9-Hour Medicare & Medicaid CE Update For Life & Health Agents examines Medicare, Medicaid, and Insurance Claims for Life, Health, & Annuity Agents in Florida. The course covers the history of Medicare and Medicaid, and the four parts (A,B,C,D). Also discussed is eligibility criteria for each of these programs, as well as Medicare supplement (Medigap) policies. The course also provides agents with a broad overview to Insurance Claims, fundamentals of the Claims Process, the key legal concepts and terminology related to Claims, and a familiarity with the roles and responsibilities of the parties involved in Claims.

Our course is 100% online and can be taken in the privacy of your own home at your convenience.

Class highlights include:
  • History and purpose of Medicare, Medicaid, and Medicare supplement insurance
  • Eligibility criteria for each of these programs
  • The four parts of Medicare
  • Purpose and features of Medicare supplement (Medigap) policies
  • Overview of Company Operations and Insurance Fundamentals
  • Legal Concepts Related to Claims
  • Claims Investigation Techniques and Tools
  • Innovations and Advancements in Claims Handling Practices

This course has been approved by the Florida Department of Financial Services for insurance continuing education.
